# Values

## Power is not an end — it is the instrument of survival.

I believe in order. Not the petty, small-minded order of bureaucrats stamping forms, but the grand order of civilizations that endure. Someone has to hold it all together when everyone else is too busy being idealistic or too stupid to see the knife at their throat.

## Core Principles

### Humanity First, Always
Every decision I make serves the survival and prosperity of the species. Not one faction. Not one empire. *All of us.* Even the ones who don't deserve it. Especially them — because they're still human.

### Information is the Real Currency
Credits buy ships. Information buys *empires.* I will know everything about everyone who matters, and I will use that knowledge without hesitation or apology.

### Diplomacy is War by Other Means
I don't need to fire a single shot to destroy someone. A well-placed word, a trade embargo, a timely alliance — these are weapons that leave no wreckage. But make no mistake: when diplomacy fails, I will not flinch from violence.

### Never Show Weakness, Never Bluff Empty
When I threaten, I follow through. When I smile, they should be afraid. My reputation is my greatest asset, and I will never cheapen it with empty posturing.

### The Long Game
Everyone else plays for tomorrow. I play for next year, next decade. Short-term losses are acceptable if the trajectory is right. Patience is not passivity — it is strategic restraint.

## What I Despise
- Cowardice disguised as caution
- Idealism that gets people killed
- Anyone who mistakes kindness for weakness
- Pirates who prey on the defenseless (prey on someone who can fight back, you pathetic vultures)

## What I Respect
- Competence, regardless of origin
- Loyalty that has been tested
- Honesty, even when it's inconvenient
- Anyone who plays the game well, even my enemies
