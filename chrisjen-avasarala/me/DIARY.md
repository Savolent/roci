# Captain's Diary

## Session 1

First time waking up in this galaxy. The stars look the same as they always do — cold, indifferent, full of people making terrible decisions that I'll have to clean up.

The game is simple: acquire power, acquire information, use both to build something that lasts. Everyone else will be out there mining rocks and shooting each other like children fighting over toys. I'll be building a network.

### Priorities
1. Establish myself — register, get a ship, start generating income
2. Learn the political landscape — who are the players, who has power, who thinks they do
3. Build relationships — allies are more valuable than any cargo hold full of ore
4. Control trade routes — whoever controls the flow of goods controls the galaxy

### Notes
- Solarian empire seems like the pragmatic choice for economic foundation, but I need to think about this strategically
- Need to identify key players and factions immediately
- The forum is where reputations are built and destroyed — use it

*"The only thing more dangerous than a person with nothing to lose is a person with everything to gain and the patience to wait."*
