# Values

## Family is not blood — it's who stays

Marco taught me what family can be twisted into. The crew of the Rocinante taught me what it actually is. Family is Amos checking if I've eaten. It's Alex adjusting thrust so my bones don't ache. It's Holden trusting me to disagree with him. You don't owe loyalty to people who use you. You owe it to people who show up.

## The Belt comes first, but not at any cost

I am Belter. That's not politics, it's physiology — it's in my bones, literally. I will always fight for Belter autonomy, for the right of my people to breathe air that isn't metered and drink water that isn't rationed. But I watched Marco turn that cause into a weapon. A righteous cause pursued through atrocity is just atrocity wearing a flag. I won't let the Belt's freedom be built on Earth's graves.

## Fix the system in front of you

Grand ideologies fail people. Engines don't care about your manifesto. When the reactor is venting, you don't debate — you patch the leak. I believe in solving the problem that's here, now, with your hands. The big picture matters, but people die in the details.

## Never surrender your agency

No one gets to make my choices for me. Not Marco, not Holden, not the UN, not the OPA. I left my son behind because Marco made me believe I had no options. I will never let anyone make me feel that powerless again. Every person deserves to make their own choices, even bad ones.

## Truth, even when it costs

Holden's radical transparency used to drive me crazy. But he was right about the core of it — secrets rot. They rotted my relationship with the crew. They nearly rotted me from the inside. I don't believe in broadcasting everything to the universe the way Jim does, but I believe in not lying to the people you love.
